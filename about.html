Our story

Why we do this

Desert Bloom Riding was created from a love of horses and a desire to create a place where children can feel supported, build confidence, and enjoy a positive riding experience.

We want riding to feel exciting and meaningful while still staying calm, safe, and welcoming for beginners.

Our approach

How we teach

We believe riding lessons should feel encouraging, patient, and child-focused. Every rider is different, so lessons should move at a pace that helps them feel successful and comfortable.

Patient Teaching

We want riders to feel supported, not rushed.

Confidence Building

Respect for Horses
